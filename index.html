Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

WebWorth pricing analyzer is currently a local prototype. Website URLs, pricing inputs, and selected files are processed in the browser and are not sent to a server by this static demo.

Information you provide

You may enter a URL, project duration, experience level, complexity, client region, exchange rate, and an optional screen recording. In this prototype, these inputs are used only to calculate the visible estimate.

Future premium services

If cloud accounts, saved workspaces, payments, or AI analysis are added later, this policy should be updated before launch to explain storage, vendors, retention, user rights, deletion, support, and security controls.

User responsibilities

You are responsible for checking final quotes, contracts, tax treatment, invoices, payment method costs, and compliance obligations with qualified professionals and official processor documentation.

Uploads and rights

Only upload recordings or materials you have permission to use. Do not upload confidential client material unless your agreement allows it and the production version has appropriate security controls.

Premium features shown in this prototype are coming soon. They do not create a promise of availability, pricing, timeline, or functionality until officially launched.
